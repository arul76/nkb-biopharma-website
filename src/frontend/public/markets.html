Markets We Serve

Delivering biopharmaceutical solutions across diverse therapeutic and geographic markets

Our Market Reach

NKB Biopharma operates across a broad spectrum of markets, partnering with healthcare institutions, distributors, and governments in over 30 countries. Our market strategy is built on deep scientific expertise, strong regulatory intelligence, and genuine patient-centricity.

Hospital & Tertiary Care Networks

NKB Biopharma's oncology and specialty biologics are primarily distributed through leading tertiary care hospitals and academic medical centers. We collaborate with institutional pharmacy committees and tumor boards to ensure our therapies are accessible to the patients who need them most within major hospital networks across India, South-East Asia, and the Middle East.

Specialty Clinics & Outpatient Centers

Rheumatology, cardiology, and immunology clinics represent a growing segment for NKB's subcutaneous biologic therapies. We partner with specialty clinic networks to provide patient support programs, training for healthcare professionals, and streamlined supply-chain solutions tailored to outpatient settings.

International & Export Markets

NKB Biopharma holds regulatory approvals and distribution agreements in over 30 countries spanning South Asia, ASEAN, the GCC, and select European markets. We work with experienced regulatory affairs partners in each market to navigate local approval pathways and adapt our labeling, patient materials, and support services to local requirements.

Research Institutions & Academic Partners

We collaborate with leading universities, research institutes, and teaching hospitals on investigator-initiated trials, translational research, and biomarker discovery. These academic partnerships fuel our early-stage pipeline and allow us to translate insights from bench to bedside with greater speed and confidence.

Government Health Programs & Tenders

NKB Biopharma actively participates in national and state government health programs, including the Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (PMJAY) in India and equivalent public healthcare procurement schemes in partner countries. We are committed to making biologic therapies available at affordable price points through institutional channels.

Emerging Markets & Access Programs

We believe every patient deserves access to innovative therapies regardless of geography or income level. Through tiered pricing, patient assistance programs, and partnerships with NGOs and international health organizations, NKB Biopharma is expanding access to its pipeline in lower- and middle-income countries across Sub-Saharan Africa and South Asia.
