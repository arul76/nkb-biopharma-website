About NKB Biopharma

A legacy of scientific excellence and a passion for patient outcomes

Our Story

NKB Biopharma was established with a singular purpose: to harness the power of biotechnology to create medicines that make a meaningful difference in patients' lives. What began as a small research initiative has grown into a respected biopharmaceutical company with a global footprint.

Over more than 15 years, we have built deep expertise across oncology, cardiovascular disease, immunology, rare diseases, and biologics — therapeutic areas where innovation can have the greatest impact.

Our name — NKB — represents the core values woven into every aspect of our work: Novelty in science, Knowledge-driven decisions, and Bold action for patients.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To discover, develop, and deliver innovative biopharmaceutical solutions that address unmet medical needs, improve quality of life, and extend survival for patients around the world — while maintaining the highest standards of scientific rigor and ethical practice.

Our Vision

To be a globally recognized biopharmaceutical leader known for transformative therapies, scientific innovation, and an unwavering commitment to the patients, healthcare professionals, and communities we serve — creating a healthier tomorrow for generations to come.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We operate with complete transparency and ethical responsibility in every decision we make.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of science to develop novel solutions where none existed.

Collaboration

We partner with researchers, clinicians, and organizations globally to accelerate breakthroughs.

Patient Focus

Every decision we make is guided by the patients and families who depend on the therapies we create.
