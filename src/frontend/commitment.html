Our Commitment

Upholding the highest standards in everything we do — for patients, science, and the planet

What We Stand For

At NKB Biopharma, commitment is not a statement — it is a practice embedded in every process, decision, and interaction. From the laboratory to the patient, we uphold rigorous standards of quality, ethics, and sustainability.

Quality Excellence

We adhere to the most stringent international quality standards across all our manufacturing and quality control operations.

  • GMP-certified manufacturing facilities
  • ICH Q10 Pharmaceutical Quality System
  • ISO 9001:2015 certified processes
  • Zero-deviation culture across production lines
  • Continuous process validation and monitoring

R&D Innovation

We invest significantly in early-stage and translational research to build a pipeline that addresses tomorrow's medical challenges.

  • 15%+ of annual revenue reinvested in R&D
  • Dedicated discovery biology and medicinal chemistry teams
  • Partnerships with top-tier academic research centers
  • Proprietary biologic expression and purification platforms
  • AI-assisted drug target identification programs

Patient Safety

Patient safety is the foundational principle underpinning every stage of our drug development and post-market surveillance.

  • Robust pharmacovigilance systems globally
  • Independent Data Safety Monitoring Boards for all trials
  • Transparent adverse event reporting protocols
  • Post-market registries for long-term safety tracking
  • Patient-reported outcome integration in all studies

Environmental Sustainability

We recognize our responsibility to the planet and are committed to reducing the environmental footprint of our operations.

  • Carbon-neutral manufacturing target by 2030
  • Green chemistry principles in synthetic routes
  • Zero liquid discharge facilities
  • Sustainable and recyclable packaging
  • Renewable energy adoption across all sites

Regulatory Compliance

Maintaining full compliance with global regulatory requirements is non-negotiable at every stage of our operations.

  • Regular CDSCO, FDA, and EMA inspections passed
  • Dedicated regulatory affairs teams per market
  • ICH guideline-compliant development programs
  • Proactive engagement with regulatory agencies
  • Annual internal compliance audits

Community & Social Responsibility

We invest in communities where we operate, supporting healthcare access, STEM education, and local economic development.

  • Free medical camps and patient awareness drives
  • Scholarships for pharmacy and life-sciences students
  • Local procurement and hiring prioritization
  • Partnerships with patient advocacy organizations
  • Annual CSR spend exceeding 2% of net profit

Advancing life sciences through innovation, integrity, and a patient-first approach.
