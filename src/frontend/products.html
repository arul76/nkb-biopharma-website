Our Product Portfolio

Five innovative biopharmaceutical therapies addressing critical unmet medical needs

NKB Biopharma's product portfolio represents years of rigorous research and development. Each product is designed to address significant medical challenges using cutting-edge biotechnology platforms. All products meet the highest international regulatory and quality standards.

01

NKB-ONCO 101 — Targeted Oncology Therapy

NKB-ONCO 101 is a first-in-class targeted therapy for treatment-resistant solid tumors. It employs a novel dual-receptor antagonism mechanism that selectively inhibits tumor cell proliferation while sparing normal tissues. Clinical trials have demonstrated a significant reduction in tumor volume and improved overall survival rates in patients with advanced non-small cell lung cancer and colorectal cancer.

Administration: Intravenous infusion  |  Stage: Phase III Clinical Trials  |  Regulatory: IND Approved (FDA/EMA)

OncologySolid TumorsTargeted TherapyPhase III
02

NKB-CARDIO 202 — Cardiovascular Biologic

NKB-CARDIO 202 is a next-generation cardiovascular biologic that significantly reduces the risk of major adverse cardiac events (MACE) in high-risk patients. By targeting a key inflammatory pathway implicated in atherosclerosis and plaque instability, it addresses the root cause of heart disease beyond conventional lipid-lowering approaches.

Administration: Subcutaneous injection (monthly)  |  Stage: Phase II/III  |  Regulatory: Breakthrough Therapy Designation

CardiovascularAtherosclerosisBiologicPhase II/III
03

NKB-IMMUNO 303 — Immunotherapy Platform

NKB-IMMUNO 303 harnesses the body's own immune system to combat systemic autoimmune disorders including rheumatoid arthritis, inflammatory bowel disease, and psoriatic arthritis. It is a bispecific antibody that simultaneously blocks two major inflammatory mediators, providing superior efficacy compared to monospecific biologics in head-to-head studies.

Administration: Subcutaneous injection (bi-weekly)  |  Stage: Phase III  |  Regulatory: Fast Track Designation (FDA)

ImmunologyAutoimmuneBispecific AntibodyPhase III
04

NKB-RARE 404 — Gene Therapy for Rare Diseases

NKB-RARE 404 is a groundbreaking adeno-associated virus (AAV)-based gene therapy for a subset of rare monogenic disorders including certain lysosomal storage diseases and hereditary enzyme deficiencies. A single administration delivers a functional copy of the defective gene directly to target cells, offering a potential long-term or curative treatment option.

Administration: One-time IV infusion  |  Stage: Phase I/II  |  Regulatory: Orphan Drug Designation (FDA/EMA)

Rare DiseasesGene TherapyAAVOrphan Drug
05

NKB-BIO 505 — High-Purity Monoclonal Antibody

NKB-BIO 505 is a fully human monoclonal antibody (mAb) targeting a validated inflammatory cytokine pathway for complex inflammatory and fibrotic conditions. Manufactured using NKB's proprietary cell-culture platform, it achieves best-in-class purity (>99.5%) and consistent glycosylation profiles, translating to improved pharmacokinetics and a favorable safety profile.

Administration: Intravenous infusion (quarterly)  |  Stage: Phase II  |  Regulatory: IND Approved

BiologicsMonoclonal AntibodyInflammationPhase II
